Sub Cinema Sunday Six: A Woman Under the Influence
Dustin Moskovitz was invited
This week at Sub Cinema Sunday: John Cassavetes's "A Woman Under the Influence." Representin' the Godfather of American Independent Filmmaking. If you haven't seen a Cassavetes before, expect a storytelling experience so raw and realistic you won't believe how believable it is. No glossy Hollywood bullshit here; Cassavetes smiled big and character-acted in blockbusters like The Dirty Dozen just to fund his true passions. This auteur elicits immersive performances within intense scenarios like few others can. Vegetables will be served. RAW. Starring Peter Falk (television's famous Lieutenant Columbo, as well as an ingenious turn as "himself" in Wim Wenders's Wings of Desire) and Gina Rowlands (Cassavetes's wife and ten-time collaborator). a couple scenes if you're interested: http:// www.youtube.com/ watch?v=iJcZFFh3fUU&f eature=related http:// www.youtube.com/ watch?v=6ZvekFAlGrM&f eature=related
