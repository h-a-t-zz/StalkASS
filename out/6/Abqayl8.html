ENVELOP Kickstarter Campaign Launch Party
Dustin Moskovitz was invited
ENVELOP is a 3D immersive music venue (28.4 ambisonic system) with interactive lights and video, to be installed in The Midway a creative complex opening soon in the Dogpatch district of SF. Using software we've developed, artists can seamlessly compose music that will envelop the listener with sound that moves around the audience from any direction. We believe this is the future of music production. We have currently raised enough funding for the equipment but need the community's support for the final build out. Help us launch our Kickstarter campaign so together we can make this vision a reality! Thursday, April 9th 8:00 PM - 1:00 AM Public Works, San Francisco FREE Music by Christopher Willits (DJ Set), Mikey Maramag DJ set (Blackbird Blackbird) DJ Dials, Manitous, Mophono (DJ Set), and other special guests! + Head's up that this is a stereo (normal 2 channel) event using Public Works wonderful Funktion-One system. In other words, we need to raise the money for the 3D ENVELOP experience! About ENVELOP: An intimate performance space opening Summer 2015 and housed within The Midway creative complex in San Francisco, ENVELOP is at once a unique audiovisual instrument for electronic artists, and a world-class venue for audiences to experience sound and light. Designed from the ground up for immersive electronic music and performance, the ENVELOP system comprises a next-generation 28.4 channel Ambisonic sound system, individually addressable LED lights, and high-definition video projection. The entire audiovisual system can be controlled as a single instrument using open-source software that integrates into an electronic musician or lighting artist's existing toolkit. http://envelop.us
