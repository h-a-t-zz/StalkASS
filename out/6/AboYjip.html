Flaming Lotus Girls present: Livin' la Vida Lambada!
Dustin Moskovitz was invited
Excite your inner ear with the thumping sounds of Opulent Temple and feast your eyes on the wondrous fire art of the Flaming Lotus Girls, all in support of the Tympani Lambada, the FLG's new magnificent fire art sculpture for Burning Man 2011. Tympani Lambada is a sculptural embodiment of the bone and membrane of the inner ear created on a grand scale with metal, fire, light and sound. The Opulent Temple will be setting up their full stage w their iconic DJ booth, massive visual screens, lazers, inflatables, DJ's, and more. Lineup includes: Dutch Drew Drop Billy Casazza Billy Seal Jonboy Mike Butler JB Vries Raffle prizes including one BURNING MAN TICKET! Fire art by the Flaming Lotus Girls! A half mile from the Colosseum BART We have off street parking and a bicycle parking as well $15 presale http:// www.brownpapertickets .com/event/ 186699 $20 door This is a 21 and over event, no exceptions! ******************************** ******************** ********* This is our piece of pretty from Flaming Lotus Girls this year. Besides many long hours of volunteers' time, we need help making the piece come alive. Your support buys materials, transports the piece, and supports the crew during the building process on the Playa. We hope you will come and join us, whether at the shop making the sculpture, or pushing the buttons some magical night in Black Rock City. Your contribution will earn you big karma points and our undying fiery love and kisses! Tympani Lambada is a sculptural embodiment of the structures by which we hear and balance. The bone and membrane of the inner ear’s vestibular system is translated on a grand scale with steel and fire, flame effects, LEDs and sound, creating an experience rich with visceral sensuality. ******************** ******************** ******************** * The Flaming Lotus Girls are a female-driven, volunteer-based group of artists who have been making kinetic, mechanical fire art since 2000. Our work stands at the intersection of sculpture, kinetics, robotics, pyrotechnics, and electronic technology. We create interactive large-scale fire installations that engage viewers and invite them to become part of the art. Flaming Lotus Girls’ work is a collaborative process that empowers participants to learn new skills and become experienced, talented and active artists. Through an open and supportive cultural environment, the Flaming Lotus Girls promote creativity, education, volunteer contribution and leadership opportunities.

Super Happy Block Party Hackathon
Dustin Moskovitz was invited
Bridging the worlds of software developers, artists, and the City of Palo Alto, the inaugural Super Happy Block Party Hackathon will happen in downtown Palo Alto on March 31, 2012 from 1pm - 1am. Innovation Endeavors, Talenthouse, and Institute for the Future will open their doors to the community in the spirit of innovation by creating the perfect environment for an interactive block. The City of Palo Alto will block off High Street between University and Hamilton. In addition, the day long event will feature: fleets of colorful food trucks, parking spaces dedicated to local startups, and a Hack the Future tent to teach kids to code. Super Happy Block Party is the 50th Hackathon of Super Happy Dev House — a five year standing community of hackers, software engineers and makers who join forces at monthly Hackathons. In the tradition of Super Happy Dev House, Super Happy Block Party Hackathon is partnering with Dev House’s sister projects, Hack the Future and Hacker Dojo — making ideas, learning, and curiosity contagious. Highlights of the Super Happy Block Party will include: a Day Star Zome Silent Disco with a live DJ inside; a constellation of LED strip lights laid out on the block for developers to hack; food truck culinary mash-ups, and a cavalcade of high-tech personalities and founders. Some of Silicon Valley's top investors will be holding Office Hours in an alley. Sign up to have a friendly conversation here: http://tinyurl.com/ 7yem26m Local start ups and businesses along the High Street Innovation Corridor will be showcased in parklets (parking spaces) which will debut in the Popup Innovation Parking Lot on High Street. Startups can reserve a parklet by feeding the meter after contacting us via our microsite www.superhappy.be This event is hosted by Innovation Endeavors, Talenthouse, the City of Palo Alto, Institute for the Future, and Super Happy Dev House with the support and participation of local startups. To ignite the spirit of the Super Happy Block Party Hackathon, the sponsors produced this playful movie trailer which can be viewed here http://vimeo.com/ besuperhappy/trailer For more information on Super Happy Block Party, be super happy, at http:// www.superhappy.be - hashtag #shbp / @superhappybe
