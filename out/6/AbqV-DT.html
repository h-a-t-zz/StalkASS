The Happiness Institute presents Exploring Your Happiness with Randy Taran
Dustin Moskovitz was invited
Join Randy Taran for the first of a seven part workshop series being presented by Project Happiness and The Happiness Institute. The focus of this first workshop is exploring your happiness – how do you define happiness and what steps do you take to achieving lasting happiness. EXPLORING YOUR HAPPINESS is the first in a seven part series of workshops Randy Taran will be presenting in partnership with The Happiness Institute. As with all presentations at the Happiness Institute, this event is FREE and open to the public. ABOUT RANDY Randy Taran is the founder of Project Happiness, a nonprofit organization that empowers people to access greater happiness in their lives and bring that happiness to the world. She is the creator, narrator and producer of the award-winning film, Project Happiness and the co-author of the Project Happiness Handbook, an interactive journey that illustrates best practices in social and emotional learning. Randy has developed a happiness curriculum that integrates the best in positive psychology, mindfulness and neuroscience. She speaks regularly on how to achieve lasting happiness and she is a syndicated blogger for the Huffington Post. *Please note: This event will be filmed for usage on the sponsors' web sites and may be used in other promotional, editorial and/or commercial purposes.
